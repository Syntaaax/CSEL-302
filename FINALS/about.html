We are the Intelligent Senses Company, which is creating software for helping people with disabilities, such as deaf people.

We want to address the problem of a lack of education in the deaf community. Our company developed this website, named Signguage, for enhancing and improving the communication skills and abilities of deaf people. Our goal is to create awareness for the deaf community and provide knowledge to deaf children, deaf parents, and able-bodied people.
